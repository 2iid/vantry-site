Questions

Good to know

Do I need Claude Code? ›
No. Vantry is built on the AGENTS.md open standard, so it works with Codex, Cursor, Copilot, Gemini CLI & more. Claude Code gets the fullest experience (native sub-agents + auto-invoked skills), but you get the whole method on any agent.
What exactly is Vantry? ›
A drop-in kit that gives your AI coding agent a senior engineering crew, verification-first playbooks, and its signature /adopt survey — a read-only audit of an existing repo that becomes a stabilize-first plan. Extracted from real production builds.
Will the survey change my code? ›
No — /adopt is read-only. It produces a report and a plan. Every fix afterward lands as a PR, never a silent rewrite, and it never touches a zone you've marked off-limits.
Which stacks does it support? ›
Any. The roles and playbooks are stack-agnostic; for anything the core doesn't cover, Vantry forges the specialist you need — web, mobile, game, data, embedded.
Updates and refunds? ›
Lifetime updates included, and a 14-day no-questions refund.
